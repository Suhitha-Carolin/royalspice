About Our Restaurant

Discover the story behind Vizag's favorite Indian dining destination

Traditional Recipes, Modern Experience

Founded in 2010 by Chef Ramesh Naidu, Royal Spices began as a small family restaurant in Vizag. Chef Ramesh brought with him generations of culinary knowledge passed down from his ancestors who served in royal kitchens of Andhra Pradesh.

What started as a modest eatery serving authentic Andhra-style cuisine has now grown into Vizag's most celebrated restaurant known for its commitment to traditional cooking methods and high-quality local ingredients.

We still prepare all our dishes the old-fashioned way - grinding spices fresh daily, slow-cooking curries to perfection, and baking bread in our traditional tandoor oven.

Our Vizag Connection

Located in the heart of Vizag, we take pride in sourcing our seafood fresh from the Vizag fishing harbor daily. Our special Andhra seafood curries feature the catch of the day prepared with traditional coastal spices.

We're proud to be part of Vizag's vibrant food scene and contribute to the city's reputation for excellent coastal Andhra cuisine.
